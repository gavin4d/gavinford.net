Gavin Ford

Gavin Ford is an undergraduate student at Worcester Polytechnic Institute (WPI) pursuing a B.S./M.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) and is expected to graduate in 2026. He gained electronics and PCB design experience through an early college program at Portland Community College and obtained additional electrical design experience through developing radio direction finding systems and translational drift robots at WPI. He is interested in pursuing work in radio frequency and analog design.
